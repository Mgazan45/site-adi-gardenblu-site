About Us?

Founded in 2022, our company operates in the agriculture and food industry, with a primary focus on the processing of fruits and vegetables. As Garden Blu Tarım, we adopt quality, hygiene, and sustainability as our core principles at every stage of production. We aim to provide healthy and reliable products while preserving natural resources, combining traditional agricultural values with modern technologies. With a wide product range, including pickles, brined products, and processed plant-based foods, we contribute to both local and national markets. Each of our products goes through carefully managed production processes to preserve its natural quality before reaching your table. With a customer-oriented approach, we strive to build trustworthy partnerships and leave a lasting mark in the industry. As Garden Blu Tarım, we continue to create value for the future, inspired by nature.
